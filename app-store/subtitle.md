# Subtitle

## Purpose

The subtitle appears below the app name in the Shopify App Store.

It should clearly explain what StoreFlow does in one short sentence.

---

## Recommended Subtitle (Version 1)

Wholesale operations platform for Shopify.

---

## Alternative Options

### Option A

Manage wholesale operations with confidence.

---

### Option B

Wholesale order rules made simple.

---

### Option C

Manage wholesale purchasing with ease.

---

## Recommendation

**Wholesale operations platform for Shopify.**

Reason:

- Clearly communicates product positioning.
- Matches the long-term vision of StoreFlow.
- Does not limit future product expansion.
- Aligns with the Product Foundation.
