# Wholesale operations, simplified.

StoreFlow helps Shopify wholesale merchants manage purchasing rules, customer access, and ordering workflows from one unified platform.

---

## Why StoreFlow

Running a wholesale business often requires multiple apps to manage purchasing rules, customer permissions, and ordering workflows.

StoreFlow brings these essential tools together into one platform, making wholesale operations simpler, more consistent, and easier to manage.

---

### Order Policies

Create purchasing rules that fit your wholesale business.

- Minimum Order

- Collection MOQ

### Customer Access
