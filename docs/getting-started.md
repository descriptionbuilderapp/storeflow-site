# Getting Started

Welcome to StoreFlow.

StoreFlow helps Shopify wholesale merchants manage purchasing rules, customer access, and wholesale ordering workflows from one unified platform.

This guide walks you through the recommended setup process.

---

# Before You Begin

Before configuring StoreFlow, make sure:

- Your Shopify store is active.
- StoreFlow is installed.
- The Theme App Extension is enabled.
- Required Shopify permissions have been granted.

---

# Recommended Setup Order

To get the best experience, configure StoreFlow in the following order.

## Step 1 — Minimum Order

Set your store-wide minimum order amount if required.

---

## Step 2 — MOQ Rules

Configure product, variant, or collection minimum quantities.

---

## Step 3 — Customer Access

Choose who can:

- View prices
- Add products to cart
- Purchase products

---

## Step 4 — Quick Order

Enable Quick Order to improve the wholesale purchasing experience.

---

## Step 5 — Verify Your Configuration

Test your storefront using a customer account that matches your expected purchasing rules.

Confirm:

- Purchasing rules work correctly
- Customer restrictions behave as expected
- Checkout validation is applied
