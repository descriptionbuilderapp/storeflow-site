# StoreFlow Brand Guide

**Version:** 1.0

---

# Brand Position

StoreFlow is a professional Shopify app designed for wholesale merchants.

The brand should communicate trust, simplicity, and operational efficiency.

---

# Brand Personality

StoreFlow should always feel:

- Professional
- Reliable
- Modern
- Simple
- Helpful

Avoid sounding:

- Overly technical
- Aggressive
- Sales focused
- Complicated

---

# Brand Voice

Write clearly and directly.

Focus on business value rather than technical implementation.

Prefer words such as:

- Manage
- Configure
- Control
- Simplify
- Improve

Avoid words such as:

- Ultimate
- Revolutionary
- Incredible
- Powerful
- Best Ever

---

# Primary Tagline

Wholesale operations, simplified.

---

# Secondary Message

A wholesale operations platform for Shopify.

---

# Writing Style

Use short sentences.

Use descriptive headings.

Explain benefits before features.

Prefer:

"Control customer access."

Instead of:

"Hide prices with customer tag visibility rules."

---

# UI Writing

Buttons should be action-oriented.

Examples:

- Save
- Continue
- Enable
- Disable
- View Rules
- Add Rule
- Create Rule Set

Avoid vague labels such as:

- Submit
- Confirm
- Execute

---

# Screenshot Style

Screenshots should demonstrate business outcomes instead of individual settings.

Every screenshot should answer one question:

"What problem does this solve?"

---

# Design Principles

Keep the interface:

- Clean
- Consistent
- Easy to scan
- Minimal

Do not overload pages with excessive text or controls.

---

# Brand Promise

StoreFlow helps wholesale merchants manage wholesale operations with confidence.
