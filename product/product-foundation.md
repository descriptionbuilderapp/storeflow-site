# Product Overview

## What is StoreFlow?

StoreFlow is a wholesale operations platform for Shopify.

It helps wholesale merchants manage purchasing rules, customer access, and ordering workflows from a single platform.

Instead of installing multiple apps to solve different wholesale problems, StoreFlow brings the essential operational tools together into one unified solution.

---

## What StoreFlow Is Not

StoreFlow is not:

- A Minimum Order app
- An MOQ app
- A Hide Prices app
- A Quick Order app

These are features.

StoreFlow is a wholesale operations platform.
