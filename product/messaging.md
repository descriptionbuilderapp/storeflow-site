# Product Messaging

## Product Name

StoreFlow

---

## Primary Positioning

A wholesale operations platform for Shopify.

This is the primary positioning statement used across the website, documentation, and Shopify App Store.

---

## Brand Promise

Simplify wholesale operations without adding complexity.

---

## Core Message

StoreFlow helps Shopify wholesale merchants manage purchasing rules, customer access, and wholesale ordering workflows from one unified platform.

---

## Three Key Benefits

### Control Your Wholesale Rules

Manage minimum orders, MOQ requirements, and purchasing policies from one place.

---

### Control Customer Access

Decide who can view prices, add products to the cart, and complete checkout.

---

### Improve Wholesale Ordering

Provide a faster and more consistent ordering experience for wholesale customers.

---

## What Makes StoreFlow Different

StoreFlow combines multiple wholesale management capabilities into one platform instead of requiring merchants to install and maintain several separate apps.

---

## Brand Personality

StoreFlow should always sound:

- Professional
- Clear
- Reliable
- Helpful
- Confident

Avoid sounding:

- Aggressive
- Technical
- Overly promotional
- Complicated

---

## Writing Style

Use clear and concise language.

Focus on business outcomes rather than technical implementation.

Prefer:

- "Manage"
- "Control"
- "Simplify"
- "Configure"

Avoid:

- "Powerful"
- "Ultimate"
- "Best Ever"
- "Revolutionary"

---

## Product Tagline

Primary:

**Wholesale operations, simplified.**

Alternative:

**Manage wholesale with confidence.**

---

## Elevator Pitch

StoreFlow is a wholesale operations platform for Shopify that helps merchants manage purchasing rules, customer access, and wholesale ordering workflows from one unified platform.
