# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 2026

## Introduction

StoreFlow ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information StoreFlow collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

---

## Information We Collect

StoreFlow only collects the information necessary to provide and maintain the application.

Depending on how StoreFlow is used, this may include:

- Shopify store information
- Store configuration and settings
- Customer tags used for rule evaluation
- Product, variant, and collection identifiers
- App preferences

StoreFlow does not collect customer payment information.

StoreFlow does not collect customer passwords.

---

## How We Use Information

Information is used only to:

- Provide StoreFlow functionality
- Save merchant settings
- Apply purchasing rules
- Improve application reliability
- Diagnose technical issues

---

## Data Sharing

StoreFlow does not sell merchant data.

StoreFlow does not share merchant data with third parties except when required to provide the service or comply with applicable law.

---

## Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used to help protect merchant information.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

---

## Data Retention

StoreFlow retains information only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

---

## Your Rights

Merchants may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their StoreFlow data where applicable.

---

## Shopify

StoreFlow operates on the Shopify platform and follows Shopify's requirements for app developers.

Merchants should also review Shopify's own Privacy Policy.

---

## Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

The latest version will always be available on this page.

---
